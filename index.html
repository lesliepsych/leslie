About Me
Leslie A. Esperida
Registered Psychometrician Certified Mental Health First Aider BS Psychology

I'm a passionate mental health advocate with a deep commitment to making psychological well-being accessible and understood. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Adamson University, where I built a strong foundation in understanding human behavior and mental processes.

My mission is to bridge the gap between academic psychology and everyday life — empowering individuals through knowledge, awareness, and compassion.

  • Land Bank of the Philippines Human Resources Internship — Practical experience in organizational psychology and HR processes
